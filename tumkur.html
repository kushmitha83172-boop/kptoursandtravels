Mandaragiri Jain Temple
Mandaragiri Jain Temple is a peaceful pilgrimage site known for its striking peacock-feather-shaped Guru Mandir. Located on a small hill, it offers panoramic views and a calm spiritual atmosphere for visitors.
Mandaragiri Jain Temple

A serene hilltop temple with stunning views and peaceful vibes.

Madhugiri Fort
Madhugiri Fort is built on one of Asia’s largest monolithic hills and is famous for its challenging trek. The fort features ancient gateways, ruins, and breathtaking views from the summit..
Madhugiri Fort

a historic hill fort with a thrilling trek and spectacular views from the top.

Devarayanadurga Hills
Devarayanadurga Hills is a scenic hill station known for its temples and lush greenery. It is a popular trekking and pilgrimage destination with beautiful sunrise and sunset views..
Devarayanadurga Hills

a scenic hill getaway known for its temples, greenery, and peaceful views.

Siddaganga Mutt
Siddaganga Mutt is a renowned spiritual and educational institution founded by Sri Shivakumara Swamiji. It is respected for its free education and food services to thousands of students.
Siddaganga Mutt

A sacred place known for service and spirituality.

Jayamangali Blackbuck Reserve
Jayamangali Blackbuck Reserve is a protected grassland area known for its population of blackbucks. It is a peaceful wildlife destination ideal for nature lovers and photographers.
Jayamangali Blackbuck Reserve

A protected grassland famous for blackbucks..

Markonahalli Dam
Markonahalli Dam is built across the Shimsha River and is surrounded by scenic landscapes. It is a quiet picnic spot, especially beautiful during the monsoon season
Markonahalli Dam

A scenic dam perfect for nature and relaxation

Namada Chilume
Namada Chilume is a natural spring believed to have mythological significance linked to Lord Rama. The serene forest surroundings make it a refreshing and sacred place to visit .
Namada Chilume

A sacred spring famed for its holy waters.

Siddara Betta
Siddara Betta is a rocky hill known for its caves and trekking trails. It is famous for sunrise views and attracts adventure enthusiasts and pilgrims alike.
Siddara Betta

A Thrilling hill trek with stunning views and caves

Akshayakalpa Organic
Akshayakalpa Organic Farm is a model organic dairy farm promoting sustainable agriculture practices. Visitors can learn about eco-friendly farming, organic milk production, and rural entrepreneurship.
Akshayakalpa Organic

a farm dedicated to fresh, organic, and sustainable dairy products.

Hutridurga Fort
Hutridurga is an ancient hill fort surrounded by thick forests and rocky terrain. It is a popular trekking destination offering scenic views and a glimpse into Karnataka’s historic past.
Hutridurga Fort

A Historic hill fort offering panoramic views and a memorable trek
