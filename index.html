Mysuru Inside Places

🏰 Mysore Palace (Amba Vilas Palace)
One of India’s most magnificent royal residences and the most visited monument in Karnataka after the Taj Mahal.
Mysore Palace

Royal heritage and grandeur.

Official name: Sri Chamarajendra Zoological Gardens. One of the oldest and most well-maintained zoos in India, famous for its clean layout, wide enclosures, and conservation efforts. Initially created as a private royal menagerie, later opened to the public.
Mysore Zoo

India’s oldest zoological garden.

Chamundi Hills rises about 1,000 meters above sea level and offers a full panoramic view of Mysuru city. dedicated to Goddess Chamundeshwari,The hill also features a 16-foot tall monolithic Nandi statue, carved from a single rock, which is one of the largest of its kind in India.
Chamundi Hills

Divine temple and panoramic views.

Mysuru Nearby Places

Coorg, also called Kodagu, is a beautiful hill region in Karnataka known for its coffee plantations and misty hills. It is known as "Scotland of India"
Coorg

Scenic plantations and waterfalls.

Ooty (Udhagamandalam), the "Queen of Hill Stations" in Tamil Nadu, is a premier tourist destination nestled in the Nilgiri Hills at 2,240 meters. It is known for its cool climate, tea gardens, and beautiful Nilgiri hills.
Ooty

Queen of Hills with lakes and gardens.

Male Mahadeshwara Hills (MM Hills) is a renowned spiritual and natural destination in Karnataka's Chamarajanagar district, situated near Mysore
MM Hills

Divine, Wildlife and adventure trails.
