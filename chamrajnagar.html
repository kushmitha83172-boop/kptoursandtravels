Chamarajanagar Packages

Muthathi Forest
Lies along the banks of the Cauvery River. It is known for its natural beauty, wildlife, and adventure activities. The area is ideal for nature lovers and short treks
Muthathi forest

Nature’s calm and beauty at Muthathi.

Bharachukki Falls
one of the twin waterfalls of Shivanasamudra. The wide cascading water creates a breathtaking view, especially during monsoon. It is a popular tourist and picnic spot.
Bharachukki Falls

Where water flows with breathtaking beauty.

Biligirirangana Hills
(BR Hills) is a scenic hill range known for its forest reserve and wildlife. It connects the Western and Eastern Ghats, making it rich in biodiversity. The hills offer trekking, wildlife spotting, and spiritual experiences.
Biligirirangana Hills

A perfect blend of nature and spirituality.

Himavad Gopalaswamy Temple
It is located at a high altitude in the BR Hills. The temple is often covered in mist, creating a magical atmosphere. It is dedicated to Lord Krishna and offers panoramic views of the forest.
Himavad Gopalaswamy Temple

Blessings from the hilltop temple..

Bandipur National Park
Is a famous wildlife sanctuary known for its rich biodiversity. It is home to elephants, tigers, deer, and many bird species. The forest safari offers visitors a chance to experience wildlife in their natural habitat.
Bandipur national park

Into the heart of nature at Bandipur.

Chamarajeshwara Temple
An ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva. It is an important spiritual center in Chamarajanagar. The temple architecture reflects traditional South Indian style.
Chamarajeshwara temple

A place of devotion and peace.

Ramalingeshwara Temple
Is a historic temple dedicated to Lord Shiva. It is known for its peaceful surroundings and traditional architecture. Devotees visit the temple seeking blessings and spiritual calmness.
Ramalingeshwara Temple

Blessings and serenity at Sri Ramalingeshwara Temple.

Balemantapa
A heritage structure reflecting traditional architecture. It is often associated with local history and cultural significance. The place attracts visitors interested in history and photography.
Balemantapa

A peaceful viewpoint surrounded by nature.

MM Hills
It is a famous pilgrimage and hill destination dedicated to Lord Mahadeshwara. Surrounded by forested hills, it attracts thousands of devotees every year. The place is known for its peaceful and spiritual environment.
Male Mahadeshwara Temple

Devotion meets nature at this sacred hill temple..

Huligana Maradi
It is a scenic hill viewpoint near Mandya. It is popular for trekking and watching beautiful sunsets. The hill offers panoramic views of the surrounding countryside.
Huligana Maradi

A peaceful hilltop temple with stunning views.

Maradigudda
A peaceful hill area known for its natural beauty. It is a quiet place suitable for short hikes and relaxation. Visitors enjoy the cool breeze and scenic landscapes.
Maradigudda

A hidden gem of natural beauty.

Trayambakapura
It is a historical and religious site with ancient temple structures. It is known for its spiritual importance and traditional architecture. The calm environment makes it ideal for devotees and history enthusiasts.
Trimbakeshwara Temple

A sacred place of faith and devotion.

Palar Border
View Point is a scenic viewpoint located on the border of Karnataka and Kerala, near the Nagarhole National Park forest area. It is a popular stop for travelers who want to enjoy the natural beauty of the Western Ghats.
Palar Border View Point

Nature’s beauty from the Palar Border View Point.

Hogenakkal Falls
Famous waterfall located on the Kaveri River in Dharmapuri District. It is often called the “Niagara of India” because of its wide waterfalls and powerful flow of water.The falls are surrounded by rocky hills and forests, creating a beautiful natural landscape.
Hogenakkal falls

Nature’s power flowing through the rocks.
