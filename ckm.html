Chikkamagaluru Packages

Chikkamagaluru Inside Places

Mullayanagiri

Mullayanagiri is the highest mountain peak in Karnataka.It offers breathtaking views of the Western Ghats and surrounding valleys.The peak is a popular destination for trekking and adventure lovers.

Mullayanagiri

The highest and most scenic peak in Karnataka.

Baba Budangiri

Baba Budangiri is a famous mountain range known for its scenic beauty.It is also a pilgrimage site dedicated to the Sufi saint Baba Budan.The area is surrounded by coffee plantations and misty hills.

Baba Budangiri

Sacred hills famous for coffee and misty views.

Hebbe Falls

Hebbe Falls is a beautiful waterfall located near Kemmangundi.The water flows down from a height through dense forests.It is a popular destination for nature lovers and trekkers.

Hebbe Falls

It is a beautiful waterfall hidden in dense forests.

Jhari Falls

Jhari Falls is also known as the Buttermilk Falls due to its white flowing water.The waterfall is surrounded by coffee estates and lush greenery.It is a peaceful place for visitors and photographers.

Jhari Falls

A white cascading waterfall surrounded by greenery.

Kalhatti Falls

Kalhatti Falls is a scenic waterfall located near Kemmangundi hill station.A temple dedicated to Lord Veerabhadra is situated near the waterfall.The place attracts pilgrims and tourists.

Kalhatti Falls

Kalhatti falls is a scenic waterfall with a nearby temple.

Kemmangundi

Kemmangundi is a beautiful hill station in the Western Ghats.It is famous for its gardens, hills, and waterfalls.The place offers cool weather and scenic landscapes.

Kemmangundi

Kemmanagundi is a hidden crown of Karnataka’s hills.

Z Point

Z Point is a famous trekking and sunrise viewpoint near Kemmangundi.Visitors can enjoy breathtaking views of mountains and valleys.It is a popular spot for photography and trekking.

Z Point

A breathtaking cliff viewpoint famous for its magical sunrise views.

Hirekolale Lake

Hirekolale Lake is a scenic lake located near Chikmagalur town.The lake is surrounded by green hills and coffee plantations.It is a peaceful spot for nature lovers.

Hirekolale Lake

A serene lake embraced by hills, perfect for peaceful reflections

Ayyanakere Lake

Ayyanakere Lake is one of the largest lakes in Karnataka.It is located near the Baba Budangiri hills.The lake provides water for agriculture and offers beautiful views.

Ayyanakere Lake

A tranquil lake tucked amidst rolling hills, ideal for quiet escapes.

Bhadra Wildlife Sanctuary

Bhadra Wildlife Sanctuary is a protected forest area rich in wildlife.Visitors can see animals like deer, elephants, and many bird species.It is a popular place for jungle safari and nature tourism.

Bhadra Wildlife Sanctuary

A lush jungle where rivers, forests, and wildlife thrive together.

Bhadra Dam
Bhadra Dam is built across the Bhadra River near Lakkavalli in Chikkamagaluru district. It is mainly used for irrigation and hydroelectric power generation. The reservoir is surrounded by beautiful forests and hills of the Western Ghats. It is a popular spot for sightseeing and photography.
Bhadra Dam

Bhadra Dam reflects the quiet beauty of water and hills.

Kudremukh National Park
Kudremukh National Park is a protected area located in the Western Ghats. It is known for its rich biodiversity, forests, and grasslands. Many animals, birds, and rare plants can be found here. The park is also popular for trekking and nature tourism.
Kudremukh National Park

Kudremukh National Park is a wild green paradise of the Western Ghats.

Kudremukh Peak
Kudremukh Peak is the third-highest peak in Karnataka. The name means “horse face” because the mountain resembles a horse’s face. It is a famous trekking destination surrounded by green hills and valleys. From the summit, visitors can enjoy beautiful views of the Western Ghats.
Kudremukh Peak

Kudremukh Peak rises like a horse’s face above endless green valleys.

Gangamoola
Gangamoola is the origin point of the Tunga, Bhadra, and Netravati rivers. It is located inside Kudremukh National Park. The place has a small cave temple dedicated to Goddess Ganga. It is both a sacred and scenic location.
Gangamoola

Gangamoola is the sacred birthplace of three rivers.

Ballalarayana Durga Fort
Ballalarayana Durga Fort is a historic hilltop fort in the Western Ghats. It was built during the Hoysala dynasty. Today the fort remains in ruins but still attracts many visitors. The place offers beautiful views of the surrounding mountains and valleys.
Ballalarayana Durga Fort

Ballalarayana Durga Fort stands as a silent guardian of the hills.

Ballalarayana Durga Trek
Ballalarayana Durga Trek is a popular trekking route in Chikkamagaluru. The trail passes through forests, grasslands, and hills. It leads trekkers to the ruins of Ballalarayana Durga Fort. The trek is known for its scenic landscapes and adventure experience.
Ballalarayana Durga Trek

Ballalarayana Durga Trek is a scenic trail through clouds and grasslands.

Hanuman Gundi Falls
Hanuman Gundi Falls is a beautiful waterfall inside Kudremukh National Park. Water flows down rocky steps surrounded by thick forest. Visitors must climb steps to reach the base of the falls. It is a refreshing and peaceful natural spot.
Hanuman Gundi Falls

Hanuman Gundi Falls crashes down with refreshing mountain energy.

Kadambi Falls
Kadambi Falls is a tall waterfall located on the road to Kudremukh. It flows down a steep rock face surrounded by green hills. The waterfall becomes very powerful during the monsoon season. It can easily be seen from the roadside by travelers.
Kadambi Falls

Kadambi Falls pours like silver through the forest rocks.

Sringeri Sharada Peetham
Sringeri Sharada Peetham is an important Hindu monastery founded by Adi Shankaracharya. It is located on the banks of the Tunga River in Sringeri. The temple is dedicated to Goddess Sharada, the goddess of knowledge. It is a major center for spiritual learning and devotion.
Sringeri Sharada Peetham

Sringeri Sharada Peetham radiates centuries of wisdom and devotion.

Horanadu Annapoorneshwari Temple
Horanadu Annapoorneshwari Temple is dedicated to Goddess Annapoorneshwari. It is located in the Western Ghats near the town of Kalasa. Devotees visit the temple to seek blessings for food, prosperity, and well-being. Free meals are served to all visitors as a special tradition.
Annapoorneshwari Temple

It welcomes every soul with divine grace and food.

Belavadi Veera Narayana Temple
Belavadi Veera Narayana Temple is a historic Hoysala temple. It is known for its detailed carvings and architecture. The temple is an important heritage monument.
Belavadi Veera Narayana Temple

A magnificent Hoysala temple known for its intricate stone carvings.

Amruteshvara Temple Amruthapura
This temple was built during the Hoysala period. It is famous for its beautiful stone carvings. The temple reflects the rich history of Karnataka.
Amruteshvara Temple Amruthapura

A masterpiece of Hoysala architecture.

Devaramane Viewpoint
Devaramane is a scenic hill viewpoint near Mudigere. It offers beautiful sunset views and landscapes. The place is surrounded by coffee plantations.
Devaramane Viewpoint

A scenic hill viewpoint with sunset views

Muthodi Forest
Muthodi Forest is part of the Bhadra Wildlife Sanctuary. Visitors can enjoy jungle safari and wildlife spotting. It is a peaceful natural destination.
Muthodi Forest

A peaceful forest known for wildlife safari

Coffee Museum Chikmagalur
The Coffee Museum displays the history of coffee cultivation in India. Visitors can learn about coffee processing and equipment. It is an interesting place for tourists.
Coffee Museum Chikmagalur

A museum showcasing India’s coffee heritage.

Manikyadhara Falls
Manikyadhara Falls is a sacred waterfall near Baba Budangiri. The water is believed to have medicinal properties. Many pilgrims visit the waterfall.
Manikyadhara Falls

A sacred waterfall believed to have healing water

Datta Peetha
Datta Peetha is a sacred pilgrimage site located in Baba Budangiri hills. It is associated with both Hindu and Muslim traditions. The place attracts many devotees
Datta Peetha

A sacred hill shrine where spirituality meets stunning mountain views.

Hirekolale Viewpoint
Hirekolale Viewpoint offers a beautiful view of the lake and hills. It is a peaceful place surrounded by greenery. Visitors often come here for photography and relaxation.
Hirekolale Viewpoint

A peaceful spot where the lake mirrors the surrounding green hills.

Mullayanagiri Trek
Mullayanagiri Trek is one of the most popular trekking routes in Karnataka. The trail leads to the highest peak in the state. Trekkers enjoy stunning views during the climb
Mullayanagiri Trek

A thrilling climb to the highest peak in Karnataka.

Kudremukh Trek
Kudremukh Trek is a famous trekking route in the Western Ghats. The trail passes through grasslands and forests. It offers breathtaking views of mountains and valleys.
Kudremukh Trek

One of the most beautiful treks in Karnataka.
