Mekedatu & Sangama
Mekedatu is a scenic gorge where the Cauvery River flows through narrow rocky cliffs, creating a dramatic natural landscape. Nearby Sangama is the confluence of the Cauvery and Arkavathi rivers, known for its peaceful riverside views and trekking routes.
Mekedatu & Sangama

stunning river confluences surrounded by rocky landscapes and natural beauty

Bilikal Rangaswamy Temple
Bilikal Rangaswamy Temple is situated atop a hill and offers breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding forests. The temple is dedicated to Lord Ranganatha and is a popular trekking and pilgrimage destination.
Bilikal Rangaswamy Temple

a hilltop temple offering serene views and spiritual calm..

Nadi Narasimha Swamy Temple
Nadi Narasimha Swamy Temple is a revered shrine dedicated to Lord Narasimha. The temple is known for its serene surroundings and spiritual significance among devotees.
Nadi Narasimha Swamy Temple

A sacred riverside temple dedicated to Lord Narasimha.

Savandurga
Savandurga is one of the largest monolithic hills in Asia and a favorite trekking spot near Bengaluru. The trek offers thrilling climbs and stunning views of forests and reservoirs from the summit.
Savandurga

It is one of Asia’s largest monolith hills, famed for trekking and panoramic views.

Ramadevara Betta
Ramadevara Betta Hills, also known as Ramadevara Betta Vulture Sanctuary, is famous for rocky landscapes and birdlife. It is a scenic trekking destination with mythological connections to Lord Rama.
Ramadevara Betta

is a scenic hill known for trekking and peaceful views

Navaneetha Krishna Temple
Navaneetha Krishna Temple in Melukote is dedicated to Lord Krishna in his child form. The temple is admired for its peaceful ambiance and traditional architecture.
Navaneetha Krishna Temple

A peaceful temple dedicated to Lord Krishna.

Bidarakatte Trek
Bidarakatte Trek is a lesser-known trekking destination surrounded by lush greenery and rocky hills. It offers a quiet and refreshing escape for adventure lovers.
Bidarakatte Trek

a quiet trekking trail surrounded by nature.

Kadu refers to forested regions in Karnataka that are rich in biodiversity and natural beauty. These areas provide serene environments ideal for nature walks and wildlife exploration

Shree Revanasiddeshwara Temple
Shree Revanasiddeshwara Temple is located atop a hill and dedicated to Lord Shiva. The temple is popular among trekkers and devotees for its spiritual atmosphere and scenic surroundings.

Chunchi Falls
Chunchi Falls is a picturesque waterfall formed by the Arkavathi River. Surrounded by rocky terrain and greenery, it is a popular weekend getaway for nature enthusiasts.
