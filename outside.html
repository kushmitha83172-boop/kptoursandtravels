Mandya Places

Adichunchanagiri

Adichunchanagiri is a sacred hill destination known for the ancient Kalabhairaveshwara Temple and the Adichunchanagiri Math. Surrounded by rocky hills and greenery, it is an important spiritual and educational center in Karnataka.

Adichunchanagiri

Adichunchanagiri is a spiritual hill center with a famous monastery and temple.

Kokkare Bellur Bird Sanctuary

Kokkare Bellur is a unique village bird sanctuary famous for the nesting of painted storks and spot-billed pelicans. The local community actively protects these migratory birds, making it a beautiful example of harmony between humans and nature.

Kokkare Bellur Bird Sanctuary

It is a charming village where painted storks nest among locals’ homes.

Pandavapura

Pandavapura is a scenic town surrounded by hills, sugarcane fields, and lakes. It is known for its mythological connection to the Pandavas and offers peaceful countryside views.

Pandavapura

Pandavapura is a historic town linked to the Pandavas of Mahabharata.

Chamarajanagara Places

Bandipur National Park
Is a famous wildlife sanctuary known for its rich biodiversity. It is home to elephants, tigers, deer, and many bird species. The forest safari offers visitors a chance to experience wildlife in their natural habitat.
Bandipur national park

Into the heart of nature at Bandipur.

Bharachukki Falls
one of the twin waterfalls of Shivanasamudra. The wide cascading water creates a breathtaking view, especially during monsoon. It is a popular tourist and picnic spot.
Bharachukki Falls

Where water flows with breathtaking beauty.

Biligirirangana Hills
(BR Hills) is a scenic hill range known for its forest reserve and wildlife. It connects the Western and Eastern Ghats, making it rich in biodiversity. The hills offer trekking, wildlife spotting, and spiritual experiences.
Biligirirangana Hills

A perfect blend of nature and spirituality.

Coorg Places

Abbey Falls
Abbey Falls is one of the most popular waterfalls in Coorg, surrounded by lush coffee plantations and spice estates. The water cascades from a height of about 70 feet creating a powerful and scenic view. A hanging bridge opposite the falls allows visitors to enjoy a closer view and take photographs. The misty atmosphere and sound of rushing water make it a refreshing nature spot.
Abbey Falls

It is a beautiful waterfall near Madikeri, surrounded by lush coffee plantations and dense greenery..

Dubare Elephant Camp
Dubare Elephant Camp is a famous wildlife attraction located on the banks of the Kaveri River. Visitors can observe elephants being bathed, fed, and trained by experienced mahouts. The camp provides opportunities to learn about elephant behavior and conservation. Coracle rides on the river add an adventurous touch to the visit.
Dubare Elephant Camp

It is a popular eco-tourism spot where visitors can interact with and learn about elephants.

Karada Village
Karada Village is a peaceful settlement near popular trekking routes in Coorg. The village reflects the traditional culture and lifestyle of the Kodava community. Small homestays provide comfortable accommodation for trekkers and travelers. Its quiet surroundings make it an ideal place to relax and experience rural life.
Karada Village

It is known for its lush greenery, coffee plantations, and scenic landscapes.

Ooty Places

Rose Garden
The Government Rose Garden is one of the largest rose gardens in India, featuring thousands of rose varieties. Spread across beautiful terraces, it offers colorful views and a pleasant walking experience. It is a perfect place for photography and relaxation.
Rose Garden

Beautiful terraced garden with thousands of roses 🌹

Ooty Tea Factory and Tea Museum
The Tea Factory and Museum showcases the complete process of tea manufacturing, from leaf plucking to packaging. Visitors can learn about the history of Nilgiri tea and taste fresh tea varieties. Nearby chocolate shops and museums sell delicious homemade chocolates.
Ooty Tea Factory and Tea Museum

Learn traditional Nilgiri tea manufacturing process 🍃

Pykara Falls
Pykara Falls is a scenic waterfall surrounded by dense forests and natural beauty. The cascading water creates a peaceful and refreshing atmosphere. It is a popular picnic and photography spot.
Pykara Falls

Scenic waterfall surrounded by dense forests 💧

Ramanagara Places

Mekedatu & Sangama
Mekedatu is a scenic gorge where the Cauvery River flows through narrow rocky cliffs, creating a dramatic natural landscape. Nearby Sangama is the confluence of the Cauvery and Arkavathi rivers, known for its peaceful riverside views and trekking routes.
Mekedatu & Sangama

stunning river confluences surrounded by rocky landscapes and natural beauty

Bilikal Rangaswamy Temple is situated atop a hill and offers breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding forests. The temple is dedicated to Lord Ranganatha and is a popular trekking and pilgrimage destination.
Bilikal Rangaswamy Temple

a hilltop temple offering serene views and spiritual calm..

Tumkur Places

Mandaragiri Jain Temple is a peaceful pilgrimage site known for its striking peacock-feather-shaped Guru Mandir. Located on a small hill, it offers panoramic views and a calm spiritual atmosphere for visitors.
Mandaragiri Jain Temple

Madhugiri Fort is built on one of Asia’s largest monolithic hills and is famous for its challenging trek. The fort features ancient gateways, ruins, and breathtaking views from the summit..
Madhugiri Fort

a historic hill fort with a thrilling trek and spectacular views from the top.

Devarayanadurga Hills
