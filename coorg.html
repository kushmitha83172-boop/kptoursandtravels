Coorg Packages

Abbey Falls
Abbey Falls is one of the most popular waterfalls in Coorg, surrounded by lush coffee plantations and spice estates. The water cascades from a height of about 70 feet creating a powerful and scenic view. A hanging bridge opposite the falls allows visitors to enjoy a closer view and take photographs. The misty atmosphere and sound of rushing water make it a refreshing nature spot.
Abbey Falls

It is a beautiful waterfall near Madikeri, surrounded by lush coffee plantations and dense greenery..

Dubare Elephant Camp
Dubare Elephant Camp is a famous wildlife attraction located on the banks of the Kaveri River. Visitors can observe elephants being bathed, fed, and trained by experienced mahouts. The camp provides opportunities to learn about elephant behavior and conservation. Coracle rides on the river add an adventurous touch to the visit.
Dubare Elephant Camp

It is a popular eco-tourism spot where visitors can interact with and learn about elephants.

Karada Village
Karada Village is a peaceful settlement near popular trekking routes in Coorg. The village reflects the traditional culture and lifestyle of the Kodava community. Small homestays provide comfortable accommodation for trekkers and travelers. Its quiet surroundings make it an ideal place to relax and experience rural life.
Karada Village

It is known for its lush greenery, coffee plantations, and scenic landscapes.

Harangi Dam
Harangi Dam is built across the Harangi River, a tributary of the Kaveri. The dam becomes especially attractive during the monsoon when its gates release large volumes of water. The surrounding landscape offers scenic views and cool breezes. Many visitors come here to enjoy peaceful evenings and photography.
Harangi Dam

It is a picturesque reservoir built across the Harangi River, surrounded by lush greenery.

Honnamana Kere
Honnamana Kere is the largest natural lake in Coorg and holds religious importance. It is associated with Goddess Honnamma and local legends. Devotees gather here during the Gowri festival to offer prayers. The serene waters and surrounding hills create a calm and spiritual environment.
Honnamana Kere

It is the largest natural lake in Coorg, known for its scenic beauty and religious significance.

Tadiandamol Trek
Tadiandamol is the highest peak in Coorg and a popular trekking destination. The trail passes through dense forests, grasslands, and scenic hills. Trekkers enjoy breathtaking views of the Western Ghats from the summit. It is especially beautiful during early mornings and clear days.
Tadiandamol Trek

It is a scenic hike to the highest peak in Coorg, offering breathtaking views of the Western Ghats.

Kote Betta
Kote Betta is the third-highest peak in Coorg and a well-known trekking destination. The trek is moderately easy and suitable for beginners and nature lovers. Along the trail, trekkers pass through forests and open landscapes. The summit provides panoramic views of nearby hills and villages.
Kote Betta

It is the third-highest peak in Coorg, It has panoramic views of the Western Ghats

Madikeri Fort
Madikeri Fort is an important historical landmark located in the center of Madikeri town. The fort has witnessed the rule of several dynasties including the Kodava kings and the British. Inside the fort, visitors can see a museum displaying historical artifacts and weapons. The structure reflects a blend of Indian and European architectural styles.
Madikeri Fort

Madikeri Fort is a historic fort in the heart of Madikeri known for its colonial architecture and rich heritage.

Omkareshwara Temple
Omkareshwara Temple is a famous shrine dedicated to Lord Shiva. The temple has a unique architectural style that combines Islamic and Gothic influences. A small pond in front of the temple enhances its beauty and peaceful atmosphere. Devotees and tourists visit the temple for prayer and quiet reflection.
Omkareshwara Temple

It is a historic Shiva temple known for its unique blend of Islamic and Gothic architectural styles

Mallalli Falls
Mallalli Falls is a spectacular waterfall located near the foothills of the Pushpagiri range. Water flows down rocky cliffs creating a dramatic natural scene. \Visitors can reach closer to the falls by descending a long flight of steps. The surrounding greenery makes it especially beautiful during the monsoon season.
Mallalli Falls

It is a majestic waterfall formed by the Kumaradhara River,It has lush hills and scenic viewpoints.

Mandalpatti Peak
Mandalpatti is a scenic hilltop viewpoint known for its breathtaking sunrise and sunset views. The journey often includes a jeep ride through rough forest roads. From the top, visitors can see clouds floating below the hills during early mornings. It is a favorite spot for photographers and nature lovers.
Mandalpatti Peak

It is famous for its misty landscapes and breathtaking panoramic views of the Western Ghats

Nehru Mantap
Nehru Mantap is a peaceful pavilion located inside Raja’s Seat garden. It was built in memory of India’s first Prime Minister Jawaharlal Nehru. The structure provides a quiet place to sit and enjoy the surrounding landscape. Visitors can admire beautiful valley views from this viewpoint.
Nehru Mantap

Nehru Mantap is a peaceful viewpoint in Madikeri offering beautiful sunset views over the lush valleys.

Raja's Seat
Raja's Seat is one of the most famous viewpoints in Madikeri. It was once a favorite relaxation spot for the Kodagu kings. The garden here is decorated with colorful seasonal flowers and a musical fountain. Tourists visit in the evening to enjoy the stunning sunset views.
Raja's Seat

Raja's Seat is where the Kodagu kings once enjoyed stunning sunset views over the valley

Somwarpet
Somwarpet is a small town surrounded by hills, forests, and coffee plantations. It serves as an important gateway to several trekking and nature destinations. The region is known for its cool climate and scenic landscapes. Many travelers use it as a base to explore nearby waterfalls and hills.
Somwarpet

It is a scenic town in Coorg known for its cool climate and beautiful hill landscapes.

Talakaveri Temple
Talakaveri Temple marks the sacred origin of the Kaveri River. The temple is located in the Brahmagiri Hills at a high altitude. Pilgrims gather here during the Tula Sankramana festival when the river is believed to emerge from the spring. A nearby viewpoint offers panoramic views of the surrounding mountains.
Talakaveri Temple

Talakaveri Temple is a sacred temple marking the origin of the Kaveri River in the Brahmagiri hills.

Chiklihole Reservoir
Chiklihole Reservoir is a calm and scenic water body located between Madikeri and Kushalnagar. The area is less crowded compared to other tourist spots in Coorg. Visitors often come here for picnics and peaceful walks. The reservoir is especially beautiful during sunset when the sky reflects on the water.
Chiklihole Reservoir

It is a serene reservoir surrounded by lush greenery, known for its peaceful views and beautiful sunsets.

Tibetan Namdroling Monastery
Namdroling Monastery, also known as the Golden Temple, is one of the largest Tibetan Buddhist monasteries in India. It is located in Bylakuppe near Kushalnagar in the Coorg region of Karnataka. The monastery was established in 1963 by Pema Norbu Rinpoche to preserve Tibetan culture and Buddhist teachings.
Tibetan Namdroling Monastery

It is a famous Tibetan Buddhist monastery, It has magnificent golden statues.

Nalknad Palace
Nalknad Palace is a historic palace located near Madikeri in the Kodagu District. It was built in the 18th century by Dodda Veerarajendra, the last ruler of the Kodagu kingdom. The palace served as a safe place for the king to stay during battles.
Nalknad Palace

Nalknad Palace is a historic palace built in the 18th century by Kodava king Doddaveerarajendra

Gaddige Raja’s Tomb
Gaddige Raja’s Tomb is a historic monument located in Madikeri in the Kodagu District. It is the burial place of the Kodava kings who ruled the region in the past. The tombs were built in the Indo-Islamic architectural style with large domes and beautiful pillars.
Gaddige Raja’s Tomb

It is a historic monument complex that houses the tombs of the Kodava kings.

Padi Igguthappa
Padi Igguthappa Temple is a sacred temple dedicated to Lord Igguthappa, the guardian deity of the Kodava people.
Padi Igguthappa

It is a sacred temple dedicated to Lord Igguthappa, the guardian deity of the Kodava people.
