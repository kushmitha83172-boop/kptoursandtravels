Ooty Packages

Rose Garden
The Government Rose Garden is one of the largest rose gardens in India, featuring thousands of rose varieties. Spread across beautiful terraces, it offers colorful views and a pleasant walking experience. It is a perfect place for photography and relaxation.
Rose Garden

Beautiful terraced garden with thousands of roses 🌹

Ooty Tea Factory and Tea Museum
The Tea Factory and Museum showcases the complete process of tea manufacturing, from leaf plucking to packaging. Visitors can learn about the history of Nilgiri tea and taste fresh tea varieties. Nearby chocolate shops and museums sell delicious homemade chocolates.
Ooty Tea Factory and Tea Museum

Learn traditional Nilgiri tea manufacturing process 🍃

Pykara Falls
Pykara Falls is a scenic waterfall surrounded by dense forests and natural beauty. The cascading water creates a peaceful and refreshing atmosphere. It is a popular picnic and photography spot.
Pykara Falls

Scenic waterfall surrounded by dense forests 💧

Nilgiri Mountain Railway
The Nilgiri Mountain Railway is a UNESCO World Heritage toy train that runs through tunnels, bridges, and misty hills. The slow journey offers breathtaking views of the Nilgiri mountains. It is one of the most memorable experiences in Ooty.
Nilgiri Mountain Railway

UNESCO heritage toy train through misty hills 🚂

Moyar River
The Moyar River flows through deep valleys and thick forests of the Nilgiris. It supports rich wildlife and adds beauty to the region’s landscape. The river area is calm and scenic.
Moyar River

River flowing through forests and deep valleys 🌿

Emerald Lake
Emerald Lake is a quiet and less crowded destination surrounded by tea plantations. The serene environment makes it ideal for nature lovers. It is perfect for peaceful walks and photography.
Emerald Lake

Quiet lake surrounded by lush tea plantations 🌄

Avalanche Lake
Avalanche Lake was formed due to a landslide and is known for its untouched natural beauty. Surrounded by mountains and greenery, it offers a refreshing atmosphere. It is also popular for trout fishing and trekking.
Avalanche Lake

Pristine lake surrounded by mountains and greenery 🌲

Shooting Point
Shooting Point is a vast open meadow famous for movie shootings. The wide grasslands and scenic views make it attractive for tourists. It is a great place to enjoy cool weather and natural beauty.
Shooting Point

Open meadow famous for movie shooting 🎬

Botanical Garden
The Botanical Garden is home to rare plants, exotic trees, and colorful flower beds. It also features a 20-million-year-old fossil tree trunk. The garden is well maintained and attracts thousands of visitors.
Botanical Garden

Colorful botanical garden showcasing nature’s beauty🌺

Ooty Lake
Ooty Lake is an artificial lake popular for boating activities. The boat house offers paddle boats, row boats, and motor boats for visitors. It is a favorite family outing spot in Ooty.
Ooty Lake

Artificial lake popular for boating activities ⛵

Doddabetta Peak
Doddabetta Peak is the highest peak in the Nilgiri Hills. It provides panoramic views of the surrounding valleys and mountains. The telescope house at the top allows visitors to enjoy a closer view of the landscape.
Doddabetta Peak

Highest peak offering panoramic Nilgiri views ⛰️

Pine Forest
The Pine Forest in Ooty is filled with tall, straight pine trees creating a cinematic atmosphere. It is a popular location for movie shoots and photography. The quiet surroundings make it perfect for a short nature walk.
Pine Forest

Tall pines whispering in the mountain breeze🌲✨.

Kotagiri Falls
Kotagiri Falls is a beautiful natural waterfall located near Kotagiri, a hill station in the Nilgiri Hills. The falls are surrounded by green forests, tea plantations, and cool mountain air, making it a peaceful tourist spot.
Kotagiri Falls

Serene waterfalls hidden in Kotagiri forests🌳💦.
