Adichunchanagiri

Adichunchanagiri is a sacred hill destination known for the ancient Kalabhairaveshwara Temple and the Adichunchanagiri Math. Surrounded by rocky hills and greenery, it is an important spiritual and educational center in Karnataka.

Adichunchanagiri

Adichunchanagiri is a spiritual hill center with a famous monastery and temple.

Kokkare Bellur Bird Sanctuary

Kokkare Bellur is a unique village bird sanctuary famous for the nesting of painted storks and spot-billed pelicans. The local community actively protects these migratory birds, making it a beautiful example of harmony between humans and nature.

Kokkare Bellur Bird Sanctuary

It is a charming village where painted storks nest among locals’ homes.

Pandavapura

Pandavapura is a scenic town surrounded by hills, sugarcane fields, and lakes. It is known for its mythological connection to the Pandavas and offers peaceful countryside views.

Pandavapura

Pandavapura is a historic town linked to the Pandavas of Mahabharata.

Srirangapatna Fort

Srirangapatna Fort is a historic fortress that once served as the capital of Tipu Sultan. The fort showcases strong stone walls, ancient gateways, and remnants of Mysore’s rich history.

Srirangapatna Fort

Srirangapatna Fort is a historic fortress with rich tales of Tipu Sultan.

Panchalingeshwara Temple

Panchalingeshwara Temple is a historic Hindu temple located near Pandavapura in the Mysuru district. It is renowned for its unique dedication to five Shiva lingas, which is rare and gives the temple its name—“Pancha” meaning five, and “Lingeshwara” referring to Lord Shiva.

Panchalingeshwara Temple

Panchalingeshwara Temple is an ancient temple dedicated to five Shiva lingas.

Edmuri Balmuri Falls

Edmuri Balmuri Falls is a small yet charming waterfall formed by the Cauvery River. It is a favorite picnic and weekend spot for families and tourists visiting the region.

Edmuri Balmuri Falls

Edmuri Balmuri Falls is a scenic waterfall surrounded by rocky landscapes.

Brahmeshwara Temple

The Brahmeshwara Temple at Kikkeri is an ancient Hoysala-era temple dedicated to Lord Shiva. It is known for its artistic stone carvings and historical significance.

Brahmeshwara Temple

Brahmeshwara Temple is an ancient temple known for its Chola-style architecture.

Shivapura Satyagraha Soudha

Shivapura Satyagraha Soudha is a memorial commemorating the freedom struggle and the hoisting of the Indian national flag during British rule. It stands as a symbol of patriotism and sacrifice.

Shivapura Satyagraha Soudha

This Soudha commemorates the 1938 farmers’ satyagraha for freedom and rights.

Thonnur Lake

Thonnur Lake, also known as Moti Talab, is a large and picturesque lake surrounded by hills. It is a popular spot for picnics, birdwatching, and enjoying tranquil sunsets.

Thonnur Lake

Thonnur Lake is a tranquil lake known for birdwatching and scenic views.

Nimishamba Temple

Nimishamba Temple is a revered shrine dedicated to Goddess Parvati. Located on the banks of the Cauvery River, it attracts many devotees seeking blessings and peace.

Nimishamba Temple

It is a sacred temple dedicated to Goddess Nimishamba, believed to grant quick blessings.

Daria Daulat Bagh

Daria Daulat Bagh is Tipu Sultan’s summer palace, built mostly of teakwood. The palace is famous for its stunning murals that depict battles and royal life.

Daria Daulat Bagh

It is the summer palace of Tipu Sultan,famous for its ornate frescoes and garden.

Gumbaz

The Gumbaz is the elegant burial chamber of Tipu Sultan, his father Hyder Ali, and his mother Fakhr-Un-Nisa. Surrounded by beautiful gardens, the structure features Indo-Islamic architecture with impressive domes and pillars.

Gumbaz

Gumbaz is the mausoleum of Tipu Sultan, Tipu’s father Hyder Ali, and his mother.

Hemagiri Falls

Hemagiri Falls is a small scenic waterfall and hill area in Mandya district of Karnataka. It is a peaceful natural spot near K.R. Pet and is less crowded compared to other famous waterfalls in the state.

Hemagiri Falls

Hemagiri Falls is a scenic waterfall nestled amidst lush greenery.

Ranganathaswamy Temple

Srirangapatna is famous for the Sri Ranganathaswamy Temple, a significant Vaishnavite pilgrimage site dedicated to Lord Vishnu in a reclining posture, known for its Dravidian architecture blending Hoysala and Vijayanagara styles.

Ranganathaswamy Temple

Ranganathaswamy Temple is an ancient temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu in reclining form.

Lakshminarayana Temple

The Lakshminarayana Temple at Hosaholalu is a fine example of Hoysala architecture. It is admired for its intricate carvings and detailed sculptures depicting Hindu mythology.

Lakshminarayana Temple

Lakshminarayana Temple is a historic temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu and Goddess Lakshmi.

Sri Kanteshwara Temple

Sri Nanjundeshwara Temple (also called Sri Kanteshwara Temple) is one of the oldest and most famous Shiva temples in Karnataka.The temple is often called “Dakshina Kashi” (Kashi of the South) because of its great religious importance.

Sri Kanteshwara Temple

Sri Kanteshwara Temple is a famous temple dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Melkote

Melkote is a historic temple town located about 50 km from Mysuru. It is famous for the Cheluvanarayana Swamy Temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu. The town also has the Yoga Narasimha Temple on a hilltop. Melkote is known for its spiritual atmosphere and beautiful landscapes.

Melkote
